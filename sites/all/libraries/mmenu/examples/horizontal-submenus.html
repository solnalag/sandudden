Horizontal submenus

This is a demo page.
Click the menu-button in the header to open the menu.

Two of the menu-items have a submenu, one of those submenus even has a sub-submenu. All submenus will slide in from the right, pushing their parent item out to the left.

Note how the plugin automatically adds a back-button above each submenu.
